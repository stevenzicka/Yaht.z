How To Play

  • Player 1 starts first! (Enter your own names if you want!)
  • Click on the button to start! Each player gets 3 rolls before they must score.
  • Click on to save the dice for the next roll in the green container!
  • Whenever the player is ready, click on the to score your desired category.
  • If a player does not have any category they can score, click on a score category you want to cross out and it will be replaced with an X.
  • When both players have scored all available categories, click either to see who won!

Good Luck!
